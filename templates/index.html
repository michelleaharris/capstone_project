Welcome to Eco-Chicago! This site aims to project energy use for different types of buildings in Chicago to determine new limits for Green Energy Permits. Green Energy Permits incentivize constructing new buldings that include energy efficient and renewable energy features. To assure that Chicago is at the forefront of energy benchmarks to alleviate global climate change, it is necessary to detemine proper standards to reach these goals for 2020.

Check out the pages for the different types of buldings to learn more.
