ECO-CHICAGO

Welcome to Eco-Chicago!

I have used a database available through the city of Chicago which reports energy usage for buildings in 2010. Click here to check out the database. In my website, you can look up the predicted energy usage of your building and compare it to LEED energy efficiency standards for similar buildings. You also have the option to instead explore data yourself look at meaningful trends.

How do you match up to LEED?

By implementing machine learning, my comparison calculator provides the average, minimum, and maxmimum energy use based on the neighborhood, size of the building complex, and age of the building. You can see a graph that compares these values to LEED energy use standards for similar energy-efficient buildings and calculates how much you could save yearly if your building became more energy efficient.

Curious about the data set?

Check out the explore page. You can choose among a number of features to compare the data and observe the trends yourself. I have subselected features that are useful or interesting that you can choose from to play around with the data. Click around and enjoy!
